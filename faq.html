Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DineSpot

What is Dine Spot?
Dine Spot is an online platform that allows customers to easily book tables at their favorite restaurants. We aim to simplify the dining experience by connecting diners with available tables and offering a hassle-free reservation service.
How do I make a reservation?
To make a reservation, simply:
  • Visit our website and select your preferred restaurant.
  • Choose the date, time, and number of guests.
  • Click "Reserve Now" and provide any additional details.
  • Receive confirmation through email or SMS.
How far in advance can I make a reservation?
You can make a reservation up to 30 days in advance, depending on the restaurant's availability.
What happens if I need to cancel my reservation?
You can cancel your reservation by logging into your account, going to "My Reservations," and selecting the cancel option. Be sure to check the restaurant's cancellation policy, as some may have specific rules or fees for last-minute cancellations.
Do I need to create an account to make a reservation?
Yes, creating an account helps us provide a more personalized experience and allows you to manage your reservations easily. It also lets you receive updates on new features and offers.
What if the restaurant is fully booked?
If your preferred restaurant is fully booked, we'll suggest alternative options nearby or allow you to join a waiting list if the restaurant offers one. You'll be notified if a spot opens up.
Can I make special requests for my reservation?
Yes, special requests like seating preferences, dietary restrictions, or other accommodations can be added during the booking process. Restaurants do their best to accommodate, but availability may vary.
How do I contact customer support?
You can contact our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website, or by emailing support@dinespot.com. We're here to help with any questions or issues.
Are there any perks for frequent users?
Yes! Frequent diners can enjoy benefits like priority reservations, special promotions, and loyalty points. Be sure to check your account for available rewards and offers.
