Introduction

Welcome to DineSpot's Privacy Policy. DineSpot ("we", "us", or "our") operates the DineSpot platform, which includes our website and related services. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have regarding your data. By using DineSpot, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to improve your dining experience.

Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as data generated through your use of our services.

👤

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you register or make a reservation.

📊

Usage Data

Pages visited, time spent, clicks, and browsing patterns to help us improve our platform.

📍

Location Data

With your explicit permission, we use location data to suggest nearby restaurants and personalize results.

🍽️

Reservation Data

Details of your bookings including restaurant, date, time, table, and guest count.

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us deliver and continuously improve the DineSpot experience.

  • Service Delivery: Process reservations, send confirmation emails, and manage your bookings.
  • Personalization: Recommend restaurants, cuisines, and offers tailored to your preferences.
  • Communication: Send booking reminders, updates, and (with your consent) promotional offers.
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our platform to improve features and performance.
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, and abuse.
  • Legal Compliance: Meet our obligations under applicable laws and regulations.

Data Protection

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it.

SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.

Password Hashing

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store your password in plain text.

Secure Storage

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Firestore with strict access controls and regular audits.

Access Control

Only authorized personnel can access user data, and only when strictly necessary.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% foolproof. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep it confidential.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data under applicable data protection laws:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes at any time.
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@dinespot.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on DineSpot.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Authentication, session management, security Session
Analytics Page views, user behavior, performance metrics 1 year
Preference Language, theme, and display preferences 6 months
Marketing Personalized offers and retargeting (with consent) 90 days

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our platform.

Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party partners to deliver our services. These partners may process your data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — Database and authentication infrastructure.
  • Payment Processors — Secure payment handling (we never store card numbers).
  • Email Services — Sending booking confirmations and notifications.
  • Analytics Providers — Aggregated, anonymized usage analytics.

All third-party partners are contractually required to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable privacy laws. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
  • Notify you by email if the changes materially affect your rights.
  • Display a notice on our website for 30 days after significant updates.
